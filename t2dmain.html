DoGet

DoGet helps you remember the things you want to get or do, using colour coding and sorting.

Items "to get" are coloured yellow, "got" items are green; unwanted items are white.

How to use it

  1. If necessary, tap the "Clear" (brush) icon to reset all items to white.
  2. Tap a wanted item to turn it yellow. Repeat for other wanted items.
  3. Tap the "Sort" icon - the yellow wanted items will appear above the white unwanted items.
  4. When you've got an item, tap that row to change it from yellow to green, and tap "Sort" - the yellow items appear first, then green, then white.

Editing

You can change any item caption: tap the "Edit" icon , then tap the item to be changed.

Tap to turn all items yellow.

Tap to turn all items white.

Tap to add a new item.

Tap to hide items from the list.

Tap to restore hidden items to the list.
